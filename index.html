About Kertai Milk

Bridging Farmers and Consumers with Quality Dairy

Our Story

Founded with a vision to support local agriculture, Kertai Milk has been a trusted name in the dairy industry since Jan 2025. We take pride in connecting local dairy farmers with consumers who value freshness and quality.

Our Mission

To empower local farmers and provide our community with the freshest, highest quality milk while maintaining sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading milk assembly point known for quality, reliability, and community impact in our region.
